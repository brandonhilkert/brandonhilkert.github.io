“I can’t differentiate my website visitors”

Showing your website visitors the same newsletter subscription form OVER and OVER is not so bad, is it? Only when the visitors are already subscribed. You’re wasting precious website real estate on offers your website visitors have already taken advantage of. Seems a little impersonal, huh?

Email newsletters are a great way to keep in touch with your audience and build trust with them over time. Once you have their trust and interest, it’s natural to offer them something more – could be an email course or whitepaper, or even a book or service you offer.

Unfortunately, once you offer more than one product, it’s hard to pinpoint where exactly your website visitors are in your sales funnel when they show up to your website.

Right now, you could be showing that visitor that’s already subscribed to your website AND email course – a discount code for your latest book. All because you know where they are in your sales funnel and where they’ve shown interest in the past. Sounds perfect, right? Sadly, you can’t find anything to help you do this.

But what if you could? What if you could provide your website visitors with special offers and target messaging depending on what they’ve seen and interacted with in the past? Could be a discount code, a whitepaper, or even a personalize message thanking them for being so awesome!

Your website visitors don’t have to be an impersonal mystery.

With my Marketing Intelligence service, you no longer have to waste precious real estate on your website for offers a visitor has already taken advantage of. Send them through your sales funnel and take knowing they’re previous behavior, so you can show them the most relevant offers for THEM.
