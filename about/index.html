About

Hi. I’m Brandon. I’m a Father, Husband, Developer and Bootstrapper.

I’ve been in the technology field for about 10 years now – the last handful doing Ruby development. I’m passionate about everything web.

At some point in there I got involved with startups and building products. While I don’t have a list of impressive acquisitions to share, I love it and can’t imagine myself doing anything else.

My goal is to inspire you to improve your products and applications.

I have a newsletter, FREE email course, and recently wrote book about building Ruby gems. I tend to write most about Ruby and Rails development, with the occasional post about internet marketing and documenting my experiences building products – I hope it inspires you.

If you’d like to chat, feel free to email me or find me on Twitter.

Thanks for stopping by and hope you stick around…
